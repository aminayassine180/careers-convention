Latest News

I`m really excited to say that this year sees the start of making the Careers Convention as environmentally friendly as possible.

This year, we have gotten rid of the printed guide in exchange for the website you are currently reading

These changes help not only the school from having to print several hundred pamphlets that will be thrown away but also the environment.

In addition all the table numbering and signage have been reused from the past few years.

To companies unable to attend

Each year we try and ensure as much variety of delegates as possible. We invite many companies, some who are unable to attend for a number of reasons and some who just don`t respond to our request.

Once again, this year we have faced the difficulty that companies have struggled to secure volunteers or to release staff due to the current uncertainty surrounding coronavirus.

If you work for a company that is not represented and you think they would like to be next year please leave the details with the prefects at the door or email julia.wilkinson@parkstone.poole.sch.uk

Many thanks.
